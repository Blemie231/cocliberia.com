Active in all 15 counties, from Monrovia to Sinoe, with growing congregations committed to New Testament Christianity.

Unity & Fellowship

One body in Christ, connected through shared mission, annual conferences, and regional cooperation.

About Churches of Christ in Liberia

The Churches of Christ in Liberia are part of the Restoration Movement that began in the United States in the early 1800s, with the goal of restoring New Testament Christianity by discarding man-made traditions, denominationalism, and creeds.

Our message came to Liberia through American missionaries, Liberian students trained in the U.S., and local evangelists. The first congregations were planted in urban centers like Monrovia and have expanded inland over the decades.

Core Beliefs

We emphasize New Testament Christianity, free from denominational control or creeds.

Biblical Authority

The New Testament is the sole guide for faith and practice. Everything we practice must have clear scriptural backing.

Scripture: 2 Timothy 3:16-17

Baptism by Immersion

Baptism is essential for the remission of sins. Candidates must understand and confess their faith in Jesus Christ.

Scripture: Acts 2:38

Weekly Lord's Supper

Observed every first day of the week (Sunday) to remember the sacrifice of Christ.

Scripture: Acts 20:7

A Cappella Singing

No musical instruments used in worship. Singing is congregational and often in four-part harmony.

Scripture: Ephesians 5:19

Autonomous Congregations

Each congregation is self-governed under Christ, with no earthly headquarters or denominational structure.

Principle: Local church autonomy

Christian Unity

We advocate for Christian unity without man-made divisions, emphasizing "Christians only" but not the only Christians.

Scripture: John 17:21
